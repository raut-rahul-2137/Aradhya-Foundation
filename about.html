About Aradhya Foundation

Our journey began with a simple idea: to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry. Aradhya Foundation was founded by two friends who saw the struggles of the underprivileged in our community. Inspired by their plight, we decided to take action.

Our Mission

We strive to provide food and essential resources to those in need, aiming to alleviate hunger and restore dignity to individuals and families. Every meal served is a step toward a better future.

Our Goals

  • To distribute food to the underprivileged at least twice a week.
  • To raise awareness about hunger issues in our community.
  • To create partnerships with local businesses for food donations.
  • To empower individuals through skill training and resources.

Join us in our mission to help those in need. Together, we can make a difference, one meal at a time.
